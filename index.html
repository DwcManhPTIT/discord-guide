Discord Guide

Welcome to our Discord server! Discord is a chat app for gamers that makes it easy to communicate with your friends and teammates. In this guide, we'll show you how to join our server and get started.

How to Join

  • First, you'll need a Discord account. If you don't have one already, you can create one for free at discord.com/register.
  • Next, download the Discord app for your device from discord.com/download.
  • Once you've installed the app, open it and log in with your account credentials.
  • Click the "Add a Server" button (the plus sign in the left sidebar) and enter the server invite link that you were provided with.
  • You're now a member of our server! You can find the server in the left sidebar, under the "Servers" section.

Getting Started

Now that you're a member of our server, here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Read the server rules and guidelines in the "Rules" channel.
  • Introduce yourself in the "Introductions" channel.
  • Check out the different channels and categories to see what they're for.
  • Join voice channels to chat with other members.
  • Use the search bar to find messages and conversations.

If you have any questions or issues, feel free to ask a moderator or admin for help. Thanks for joining our server!
